About the Project

University Complaint Portal - A modern solution for student grievance management.

Our Mission

The University Complaint Portal aims to bridge the communication gap between students and university administration by providing a transparent, efficient, and user-friendly platform for submitting and tracking complaints.

Our system eliminates traditional paperwork and manual processes, ensuring that every student's voice is heard and addressed in a timely manner.

Vision

To create a campus where every complaint is addressed with transparency, efficiency, and accountability.

Key Features

Everything you need for effective complaint management.

User Authentication

Secure registration and login system with role-based access for students and administrators.

Complaint Submission

Multi-step form with categorization, priority selection, and file upload support for evidence.

Real-time Tracking

Track complaint status with an interactive timeline showing each update and admin response.

Admin Dashboard

Dedicated admin panel with statistics, filters, and bulk actions for efficient management.

Reports & Analytics

Visual charts and statistics showing complaint trends, resolution times, and category distribution.

Notifications

Real-time alerts and email notifications when complaint status changes or admin responds.
